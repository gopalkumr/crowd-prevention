Crowd Management System

The Crowd Management System is a Python-based solution designed to effectively manage crowds in various environments. It utilizes YOLO v8, a state-of-the-art object detection algorithm, to accurately detect and track individuals within a crowd.

By leveraging YOLO v8, the system can identify potential risks and monitor crowd density in real-time. This allows for proactive measures to be taken to prevent unexpected incidents and ensure the safety of individuals within the crowd.

Some key benefits of managing the crowd include:

  • Preventing overcrowding and maintaining social distancing
  • Identifying and addressing potential security threats
  • Optimizing resource allocation and crowd flow
  • Enhancing emergency response capabilities
  • Reducing the risk of accidents and unexpected deaths
